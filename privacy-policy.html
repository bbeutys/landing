1. Introduction

BBeauty ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

We are a beauty salon located in Alphen aan den Rijn, Netherlands, specializing in laser hair removal and lash extensions. This policy applies to all personal data we process.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Appointment Data: Service preferences, booking history, treatment notes, medical information relevant to treatments
  • Communication: WhatsApp messages, email correspondence, phone call records
  • Website Data: Cookies, IP address, browser type, device information (only with your consent)
  • Payment Information: Transaction records (payment processor handles card details)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Scheduling and managing appointments
  • Providing beauty and laser treatments safely and effectively
  • Sending appointment reminders and confirmations
  • Responding to your inquiries and customer service requests
  • Maintaining treatment records and medical history (for your safety)
  • Processing payments
  • Improving our services and website
  • Compliance with legal obligations (tax, business records)
  • Marketing communications (only with your explicit consent - you can opt-out anytime)

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we process your data based on:

  • Contract performance: To provide services you've booked with us
  • Consent: For marketing communications and analytics cookies (you can withdraw consent anytime)
  • Legitimate interest: To improve our services, prevent fraud, and ensure business security
  • Legal obligation: To comply with tax laws, business record-keeping requirements, and health & safety regulations

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely with the following measures:

  • Encrypted transmission: All data sent via our website uses HTTPS/SSL encryption
  • Access control: Only authorized staff members can access your personal data
  • Secure servers: Data is stored on secure servers within the EU/EEA
  • Regular updates: We maintain up-to-date security software and protocols
  • Staff training: Our team is trained in data protection and confidentiality

Retention period: We retain your personal data for 2 years after your last appointment with us. Tax and business records are kept for 7 years as required by Dutch law.

6. Third-Party Services

We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers:

  • WhatsApp (Meta): For appointment communication and customer service
  • Google Fonts: For website typography (performance, no personal data shared)
  • Analytics providers: For website usage statistics (only with your consent)
  • Payment processors: For secure payment handling (they don't share card details with us)

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties. All service providers are contractually required to protect your data and comply with GDPR.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under EU and Dutch law, you have the following data protection rights:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"): Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object: Opt-out of marketing communications or data processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent: Revoke consent for cookies or marketing at any time
  • Right to restrict processing: Request limitation of how we use your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us (details below).

8. Cookies

Essential Cookies (no consent required)

  • Language preference: Remembers your chosen language (EN/NL/UK)
  • Theme preference: Saves light/dark mode choice
  • Cookie consent: Records your cookie preference

Analytics Cookies (requires consent)

  • Website analytics: Helps us understand how visitors use our site (e.g., Google Analytics)

You can manage cookie preferences via:

  • The "Cookie Settings" link in our website footer

For more details, see our Cookie Policy.

1. Introductie

BBeauty ("wij", "ons" of "onze") respecteert uw privacy en zet zich in voor de bescherming van uw persoonsgegevens. Dit privacybeleid legt uit hoe wij uw informatie verzamelen, gebruiken en beschermen wanneer u onze website bezoekt of onze diensten gebruikt.

  • Contactgegevens: Naam, e-mailadres, telefoonnummer

3. Hoe Wij Uw Gegevens Gebruiken

Wij gebruiken uw persoonsgegevens voor de volgende doeleinden:

  • Wettelijke verplichting: Om te voldoen aan belastingwetten, vereisten voor bedrijfsadministratie en gezondheids- en veiligheidsvoorschriften

Uw gegevens worden veilig opgeslagen met de volgende maatregelen:

  • Toegangscontrole: Alleen geautoriseerd personeel heeft toegang tot uw persoonsgegevens

  • Personeelstraining: Ons team is getraind in gegevensbescherming en vertrouwelijkheid

Wij verkopen uw persoonsgegevens NIET aan derden. Alle dienstverleners zijn contractueel verplicht uw gegevens te beschermen en te voldoen aan de AVG.

7. Uw Rechten (AVG)

Volgens de EU- en Nederlandse wetgeving heeft u de volgende gegevensbeschermingsrechten:

  • Recht op inzage: Vraag een kopie aan van uw persoonsgegevens die wij bewaren
  • Recht op rectificatie: Corrigeer onjuiste of onvolledige gegevens
  • Recht op verwijdering ("recht om vergeten te worden"): Vraag verwijdering van uw gegevens (onder voorbehoud van wettelijke bewaarvereisten)
  • Recht op dataportabiliteit: Ontvang uw gegevens in een gestructureerd, machineleesbaar formaat
  • Recht op bezwaar: Opt-out van marketingcommunicatie of gegevensverwerking op basis van gerechtvaardigde belangen
  • Recht om toestemming in te trekken: Herroep toestemming voor cookies of marketing op elk moment
  • Recht op beperking van verwerking: Vraag beperking van hoe wij uw gegevens gebruiken

Om een van deze rechten uit te oefenen, neem contact met ons op (details hieronder).
