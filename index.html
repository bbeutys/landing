Frequently Asked Questions

Is laser hair removal painful?
Modern laser technology is virtually painless. Most clients describe it as a light tingling sensation. We use FDA-approved equipment with cooling systems for maximum comfort.
How many sessions do I need?
Most clients see optimal results after 6-8 sessions, spaced 4-6 weeks apart. The exact number varies depending on hair type, color, and the treated area.
How long do lash extensions last?
Classic lash extensions typically last 3-4 weeks with proper care. We recommend a refill every 2-3 weeks to maintain the full, fresh look.
Is there a consultation fee?
No, consultations are completely free! We're happy to assess your needs, answer questions, and recommend the best treatment plan for you.
Do you offer package deals?
Yes! We have several bundle packages that offer great value. Check our "Bundles" section in the price list for combo deals on popular treatment zones.
